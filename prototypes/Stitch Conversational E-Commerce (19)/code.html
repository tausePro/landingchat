How to connect your domain expand_more

To connect your domain, you need to create a CNAME record in your domain provider's DNS settings. This points your domain to LandingChat servers.

1

Log in to your domain provider's website (e.g., GoDaddy, Namecheap, Google Domains).

2

Find the DNS management page and create the following record:

Type Host / Name Value / Points to
CNAME www
domains.landingchat.co

DNS changes can take up to 48 hours to propagate. Once completed, click the "Verify & Save" button again.
